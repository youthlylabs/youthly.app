Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 August 2025

1. Who we are

Youthly (“we”, “us”) provides the Youthly mobile app focused on longevity and wellbeing.

2. Data we process

  • Account: email, name (if provided), Apple ID if you use Sign in with Apple.
  • Usage data: anonymised or pseudonymised app events (or disabled if you opt out).
  • Wellbeing data (optional): habits, goals, journals you choose to enter.
  • Diagnostics: crash and performance data to improve reliability.

3. Purposes

  • Provide app features (coach, tracking, backup).
  • Customer support and security.
  • Product analytics and improvement.
  • Service communications (e.g., important changes).

4. Legal bases

  • Contract: to deliver the service you requested.
  • Legitimate interests: security, fraud prevention, improvement.
  • Consent: analytics/marketing notifications where applicable.

5. Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share it with trusted processors (hosting, analytics, support) bound by confidentiality and security obligations.

6. Retention

We keep data only as long as needed to provide the service, then delete or anonymise it. You can request account deletion at any time.

7. Security

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS).
  • Good security practices and access control.

8. Your rights

Access, rectification, deletion, portability, objection/limitation (subject to local law). Contact us at support@youthly.app.

9. Children

The service is intended for users aged 16+ (or local age of majority). We do not knowingly process children’s data without parental consent.

10. International transfers

If data is hosted outside your country, we apply appropriate safeguards (e.g., standard contractual clauses or local equivalents).

11. Third parties & SDKs

  • Analytics & crash reporting (e.g., Firebase) — configurable in‑app.
  • In‑app purchases via Apple; we do not receive your card details.

12. Contact

Questions or requests: support@youthly.app.

This policy may change; the most current version is always on this page.
